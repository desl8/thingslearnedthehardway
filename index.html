Things learned the hard way

A comprehensive list of my mental shortcomings


1. Elementary schools like to move the speed bumps every day

2. The washing machine is not a good wheel for your hamster

3. When the police point a radar gun at your car, you should not shoot them back

4. Pleading the second in court does not mean you can shoot the judge

5. Fireworks made with live rounds are potentially hazardous for your party guests

6. If you shoot the ghosts at the haunted house, the police get involved

7. When people said to pour liquids on a fire to put it out, they apparently did not mean gasoline

8. Pulling the metal off of the top of an avocado causes it to blow up

9. Taking the engines off the plane midflight is not a good way to improve fuel economy

10. TSA officers do not appreciate when you use their tazer on other travellers

11. Fire pits are not a good way to dispose of UXO

12. Turns out that if you sneeze on Grandma, she becomes a cloud

13. If your baby is being baptised, do not forget to take them out of the water before you leave

14. Shooting down a rescue helicopter is not a good way to alert them to your presence

15. Eating brick of pure potassium is not a good substitute for bananas

16. Pouring a water bottle in that funny box next to Grandpa's hospital bed makes him sleep for a very long time

17. When the history teacher says to write an essay on drugs, he means write an essay about drugs

18. When making desserts, do not confuse cocoa powder with coca powder

19. When the TSA officer asks you to take off your shoes, they do not want you to take off the rest of your clothes

20. Rolling a bowling ball down a crowded escalator is a great way to clear up traffic, and an even better way to get arrested

21. When somebody says their dog needs its stomach pumped, they do not mean with a shotgun
